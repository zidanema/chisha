## Q3

FINDING: The prototype APanel currently uses inline color from mock data (`chidiansha-sandbox/project/sandbox-lab/panels.jsx:232-237`) and mock taste entries carry hex colors (`chidiansha-sandbox/project/sandbox-lab/data.js:203-210`).
